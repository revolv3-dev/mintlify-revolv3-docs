## 💡 Why Revolv3?

Revolv3 helps businesses **process, optimize, and manage payments** with ease. Key benefits include:

- **Smart transaction routing** for higher approval rates  
- **Automatic fallback mechanisms** to reduce failures  
- **Multi-acquirer support** to scale globally  
- **Compliance built-in** with industry standards (PCI, PSD2, etc.)
