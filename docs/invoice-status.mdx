---
title: "Overview"
description: "After sending an auto charge invoice you should get a status back. Depending on the type of payment, the response might differ."
---

The most common status to get back instantly: **ACH Payment Type**: Failed, \*Paid **Credit Card Payment Type**: Noncollectable, Paid

After 1-5 days the ACH will stay as Paid or will be updated to Noncollectable

Here is a quick list of InvoiceStatus we send back in response
